WHY SARX EXISTS

To be the best health tool possible.

Most health tools give you a number with barely any context. Sarx was built to change that. plain language, zero friction, built for everyday people who want to actually understand their health.

Everything is designed with user experience first. If something feels like work, it gets redesigned until it doesn't.

THE PROBLEM

Other tools overwhelm or under-deliver.

Most calculators throw one number at you and leave. Sarx connects your data to real next steps, so you know not just where you are, but what to do about it.

Every tool I tried was cluttered with ads, hard to use, and gave me no real context for what the numbers meant.

THE SCIENCE

Trusted formulas. Real evidence.

Every calculation uses peer-reviewed formulas from researchers and dietitians. The Learning Library ranks content by clinical evidence tier. not trends.
